# 1. BODY COMPOSITION (RFM-BASED)

## 1.1 Metrics

* **Body Fat % (calculated using RFM)**
* **Fat Mass Lost (kg)**

## 1.2 RFM Formula (Relative Fat Mass)

### **For Males**

[
\text{RFM} = 64 - \left(20 \times \frac{\text{Height}}{\text{Waist}}\right)
]

**Notes:**

* Height and waist must be in the **same units** (cm OR inches).
* Waist measured at navel on relaxed exhale.

---

## 1.3 How to Calculate Fat Mass

### Step 1 â€” Starting Body Fat %

[
\text{RFM (Start)} = 64 - \left(20 \times \frac{\text{Height}}{\text{Starting Waist}}\right)
]

### Step 2 â€” Starting Fat Mass

[
\text{Starting Fat Mass} = \text{Starting Weight} \times \left(\frac{\text{RFM (Start)}}{100}\right)
]

### Step 3 â€” Ending Body Fat %

(Recalculate RFM using the updated waist measurement)

### Step 4 â€” Ending Fat Mass

[
\text{Ending Fat Mass} = \text{Ending Weight} \times \left(\frac{\text{RFM (End)}}{100}\right)
]

### Step 5 â€” Fat Mass Lost

[
\text{Fat Mass Lost} = \text{Starting Fat Mass} - \text{Ending Fat Mass}
]

---

## 1.4 Body Composition Score

To keep things fair, fat loss is expressed as a percentage:

[
\text{Body Comp Score} =
\frac{\text{Fat Mass Lost}}{\text{Starting Fat Mass}} \times 100
]

# 2. STRENGTH

## 2.1 Metrics

* **5RM Bench Press**
* **5RM Front Squat**
* **5RM Deadlift**
* **Push-ups (1-minute max)**
* **Plank (time to failure)**

## 2.2 Rules

* All barbell lifts must use a **20 kg Olympic barbell**.
* 5RM = heaviest load for 5 perfect reps.
* Push-ups: strict form only.
* Plank ends when hips sag or form breaks.

## 2.3 Strength Scoring

[
% \text{Improvement} =
\frac{\text{Final} - \text{Initial}}{\text{Initial}} \times 100
]

Strength Score = **average % improvement across all 5 tests**.

---

# 3. ENDURANCE

## 3.1 Metrics

* **3 km treadmill time** (0% incline)
* **Average heart rate during the 3 km**

## 3.2 Rules

* Treadmill must be set to **0% incline**.
* Smartwatch must record:

  * 3 km time
  * Average HR

## 3.3 Endurance Scoring

### **(1) Time % Improvement**

[
% \text{Time Improvement} =
\frac{\text{Initial Time} - \text{Final Time}}{\text{Initial Time}} \times 100
]

### **(2) HR % Improvement**

[
% \text{HR Improvement} =
\frac{\text{Initial HR} - \text{Final HR}}{\text{Initial HR}} \times 100
]

### **Endurance Score**

[
\text{Endurance Score} =
\frac{\text{Time % Improvement} + \text{HR % Improvement}}{2}
]

---

# 4. FINAL SCORING (Equal Weighting)

Each major category = **33.33%** of the total score.

[
\text{Final Score}=
(\text{Body Comp Score} \times 0.3333) +
(\text{Strength Score} \times 0.3333) +
(\text{Endurance Score} \times 0.3333)
]

Highest final score = **Overall Champion**.
